With tiqr you can use your smartphone for safe and secure authentication to services from SURFnet.

This application does not yet contain active accounts. To make use of tiqr you need an activated account. On www.tiqr.org you will find a list of identity providers.

After registration you will receive an activation-code and instructions for activation. During the activation process you will have to scan a QR tag. Tap the scan button when asked.
